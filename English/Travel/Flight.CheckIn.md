### Check In
1. Ticket (票)
    1. 发音: ˈtɪkɪt
    2. 释义: 
        1. n 名词: 票，入场券；(交通违章)通知单，罚款单；
        2. v 动词. 售票；
    3. 示例:
        1. She bought a ticket to Paris. (她买了一张去巴黎的票)
2. tray(托盘)
    1. 发音:
    2. 释义: 
    3. 示例:
        1. Please place in the tray. 
            - 请放在托盘里
    

### Air Ticket Check In
1. Stage 1: 
```
A: Oh No, It's four clock. I'm Late.
B: Good afternoon.
A: Hello. I'd like to Check in.
B: May I see you passport, please?
A: Sure, Here you go.  
B: Where are you traveling to ?(你想要去哪里旅行呢？)
A: I'm traveling to Paris.   (我想去巴黎旅行)
B: Do you have any luggage?  (您有行李么？)
A: Yes, I have one suitcase. (是的，我有一个手提箱)
B: Place it here, Please.    (放在这里)
B: Here is you ticket.
A: Thank you.
B: Your flight leaves from Gate A7. (您的航班从A7门起飞) (Gate: 门)
B: Boarding time is at four twenty. (登机时间是4点20分)
A: Where is Security?
B: It's over there. (在那边)(一般指近的地方，用手一指就可以看到的地方)
B: You can follow the signs. (你可以跟着标志走 (sign: saɪn 标志))
A: Great. Thanks for your help!
B: Sir! Your passport! (先生，你的护照)
```
