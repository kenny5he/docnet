## 日常对话
### 问答句
1. 对话例1:
   1. A: Where would you like to go?(你想去哪里呀)
   2. B: These are the places I would like to go(这些是我想去的地方)
   3. B: Italy、Spain、Japan
2. 对话例2:
    1. A: What can i get for you today?(今天您要点什么?)
    2. B: I would like to have a chocolate ice cream, pealse.(我想要个巧克力冰淇淋)
3. 对话案例3:
    1. What would you like to drink?(你想要喝些什么呢？)
    2. I would like to have a cup of tea.(我想要一杯茶)
4. 对话案例4:
    1. What would you like to do this weekend?(这周末你想去做啥呢？)
    2. I would like to go shopping with you.(我想和你去购物)
