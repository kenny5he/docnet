## Ordered (点菜)
1. Ordered
   1. 发音
   2. 释义
   3. 扩展:
       1. I'd like ...: 我想点
       2. I'd like to have: 我想点
       3. Cloud I have ..., Please?: 请问我可以要...吗？
       4. I'll take ...: 我要...
       5. Would you like anything? 你想要点点什么嘛？ 
       6. What would you recommend? 你有什么推荐吗？
       7. What's today's special? 今日特供是什么？
       8. What's on the menu today? 今天菜单上有什么菜？
       9. What's do you use a menu in the restaurant for? 你在餐厅用菜单做什么?
       10. Would you like to look at menu? 您要看看菜单吗?
       11. Can I get you anything else? 您还需要点什么吗?
       12. I'd like to get the check please. 我要结账。
       13. I would like to go shopping with you. 我想和你去购物
       14. She don't like to mix fruits with vegetables. 她不想在沙拉中混水果
       15. We love playing table tennis。 我喜欢乒乓球
       16. pass me the menu,please.  把菜单递给我
   4. 例句:
       1. I ordered an apple pie. 
           - 我想点一个苹果派
       2. Are you ready to Order?
            - 你准备好点什么了嘛？
       

2. Slice (片)
    1. 发音
    2. 释义
    3. 扩展:
        1. a slice of pizza: 一片蛋糕
        2. a slice of beef: 一片牛肉
        3. a slice of lemon: 一片柠檬
        4. to slice something up: 切下
        5. a piece of: 一块
    4. 例句:
        1. She had a slice of pizza for breakfast.
            - 她午餐有片披萨
        2. She sliced off a piece of sausage.
            - 她切下一块香肠。
