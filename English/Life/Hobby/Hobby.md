3. 例文3
```
We all have hobbies. but few of us can turn a hobby into a job.
(我们都有爱好。但是很少有人能把爱好变成工作。)
Meet Ke jie. He turned his hobby into a job.
(柯洁。他把爱好变成了工作。)
Ke Jie likes to play the board game'Go'. (board game'GO' 棋盘游戏)
(柯洁喜欢下围棋。)
Ke Jie was born in China in 1997 and started learning how to play 'GO' when he was just five years old.
(柯洁1997年出生在中国，5岁时就开始学习下围棋。)
It only took him five more years after that to become a professional 'GO' player.
(在那之后，他只花了五年多的时间就成为了一名职业围棋选手。)
Ke Jie became famous when he played 'GO' against AlphaGo, Google's smartest computer program that plays Go.
(柯洁因与谷歌最聪明的围棋程序AlphaGo对弈而成名。)
Although Ke Jie lost the game against the computer, he became well know all over the world.
(虽然柯洁输掉了对电脑的比赛，但他却闻名于世。)
Ke Jie has a lot of passion for 'GO'.
(柯洁对围棋充满了热情。)
This passion, Ke Jie said, is something machines don't have, and is what matters most.
(柯洁说，这种激情是机器所没有的，也是最重要的。)
```


### 语法
1. 主语 + like/likes + 动名词(doing)/不定式(to do) : 表达喜欢做某事
2. 主语 + love/loves + 动名词(doing)/不定式(to do) :表达热爱做某事

- We like to swim.  -> like 用于第一/第二/第三称复数, 用动词原型
- He likes to run.  -> likes 用于第三人称单数
